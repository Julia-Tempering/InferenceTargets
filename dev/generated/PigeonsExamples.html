Usage ​

To access the list of targetIds available in PigeonsExamples:

julia
ids = find_targetIds(PigeonsExamples)
length(ids)
4

To load one target:

julia
some_id = ids[1]
load_target(PigeonsExamples, some_id)
PigeonsExamples.IsingLogPotential(1.0, 5)
