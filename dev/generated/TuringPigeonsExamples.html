Usage ​

To access the list of targetIds available in TuringPigeonsExamples:

julia
ids = find_targetIds(TuringPigeonsExamples)
length(ids)
6

To load one target:

julia
some_id = ids[1]
load_target(TuringPigeonsExamples, some_id)
